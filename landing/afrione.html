The Hidden $2 Trillion Problem

Traditional Banking Has Failed Africa

While banks struggle to serve rural populations with expensive fees and complex requirements, African communities have built the world's most successful peer-to-peer financial network. But it's trapped in manual processes that limit its potential.

  • Trust Issues: 23% default rates when personal relationships fail
  • Manual Records: Paper-based tracking leads to disputes and errors
  • Limited Scale: Groups rarely exceed 50 people due to trust constraints
  • Geographic Barriers: Diaspora can't join home country circles
  • No Investment Access: Savings trapped without growth opportunities

AfriOne: Tontines Meet Blockchain

We're not teaching Africans new financial behaviors - we're supercharging what already works perfectly with smart contracts and digital transparency.

Smart Tontine Circles

Join or create rotating savings groups with 5 to 10,000+ members. Smart contracts automate contributions, payouts, and dispute resolution while preserving community trust.

Global Participation

African diaspora can finally join their home country tontines. Send money from London to Lagos instantly with sub-$1 fees, not the traditional $15-50.

Asset Tokenization

Turn your tontine savings into investment opportunities. Buy fractions of real estate, agricultural projects, or commodities starting at just $10.

Multi-Currency Wallet

Credit Building

Blockchain Security
